South Africa stops people in their tracks — the mountains, the wine, the wildlife, the food, the culture. But for international visitors, navigating it confidently takes local knowledge, trusted logistics, and genuine expertise.

Dutch Cape was built for exactly this. We are a Cape Town–based private touring company specialising in bespoke, guided experiences for American, British, and Norwegian travellers. We plan everything, move you safely, and make sure every moment is one you'll talk about for years.

Our Commitment

Your safety is not
a feature. It's our
foundation.

We know what concerns international visitors about South Africa. We've built every system, every protocol, and every route specifically to address those concerns — without sacrificing an ounce of the magic.

🗺️
Route Intelligence
Our operations team reviews routes daily using local intelligence, municipal briefings, and guide feedback.
  • Daily route safety assessments
  • Alternate routes always pre-planned
  • No-go zones strictly avoided
  • Local community partnerships
  • Real-time traffic monitoring
🚐
Secure Transport
Every vehicle in our fleet is privately owned, maintained, insured, and GPS-tracked at all times.
  • Discreet, unmarked vehicles
  • Live GPS shared with ops centre
  • First aid kits in every vehicle
  • Sat-phones for remote areas
  • No public transport, ever
🧭
Expert Guides
Our guides are Cape Town natives with formal training in first aid, conflict de-escalation, and local knowledge spanning decades.
  • SA Tourism Board certified
  • Wilderness First Responder trained
  • English, Afrikaans & isiXhosa
  • Background-checked & verified
🏨
Vetted Accommodation
Every property is personally visited, assessed for security, and briefed on our client protocols.
  • Direct relationships, no platforms
  • 24-hour staffed security required
  • Safe neighbourhoods only
  • Emergency procedures rehearsed
📞
24/7 Operations
A real human answers our emergency line at any hour. We maintain relationships with private medical evacuation services.
  • Cape Town ops centre always staffed
  • Private ambulance on call
  • Embassy contacts on speed-dial
  • Insurance documentation handled
📋
Pre-Tour Briefing
Before you arrive, we send our comprehensive safety guide. On day one your guide walks through everything in person.
  • Digital safety pack pre-departure
  • In-person orientation on arrival
  • Emergency card for every guest
  • WhatsApp group with ops team
